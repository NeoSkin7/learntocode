Beta Program Guide

Welcome to the LearnToCode Beta Program! Underlined below is the release cycle of the program and terms & conditions.

Release Cycle

The list below outlines the release section categories that beta releases can be classed as.

  • Pre-alpha: Releases that are lacking core functionality; such as missing pages.
  • Alpha: Releases that have core functionality but not all content; such as having all pages but missing content.
  • Beta: Releases that are being polished; such as needing updated wording or diagrams.
  • Release Preview: Releases that are lacking web-standards needed for final release; such as missing meta tags and accessibility items.

Terms & Conditions

All releases are provided as-is, with no additional documentation or pages not included in release. Releases are not finalised and are subject to change. They may be lacking important functionality, content, or web-standards.
