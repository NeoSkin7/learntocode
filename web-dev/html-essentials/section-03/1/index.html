HTML ESSENTIALS

Section 3 - Text Formatting Elements

Formatting Elements

strong and b are bold tags, whatever you enclose in it will be made bold.

em is the italics tag, whatever you enclose in it will be italisiced (emphasised).

u is the underline tag, whatever you enclose in it will be underlined.

mark is the text marking tag, whatever you enclose in it will be highlighted.

del is the strikethrough tag, whatever you enclose in it will be struck through.

sub is the subscript tag, whatever you enclose in it will be made into subscript.

sup is the superscript tag, whatever you enclose in it will be made into superscript.
