Section 6 - Colors

Colors!!

Colors are another basic concept in CSS. You've already run-into a few of their units in Section 3 and are probably confused.

Color Names

Color names are the most simple way to define colors in CSS, simply type the name of a color (e.g. black or red) and that's it!

You can find a full list of acceptable color names on the MDN (Mozilla Developer Network), click here to view it.
